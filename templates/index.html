INSPIRATION

We were inspired by the potential of AI to empower healthcare professionals by reducing repetitive, time-consuming tasks and letting them focus on what matters most: patients. Our goal was to build an AI application that automates the extraction and mapping of multi-modal clinical data to industry standards to streamline research, trial, and regulatory use.

THE PROBLEM

Manual mapping of unstructured clinical data wastes valuable time and delays patient care and drug development. Semi-automation can reduce delays, boost accuracy, and free clinical teams to focus on saving lives.

WHAT OUR APP DOES

Automates data extraction of EHR (text) and X-ray (image) data, and then infers how it can be mapped to industry standards for downstream research, trial, and regulatory use.

HOW OUR APP WORKS

Extract EHR insights and X-ray data with Cohere, predict and evaluate mappings to SDTM domains, then output an industry-ready CSV/JSON file.
